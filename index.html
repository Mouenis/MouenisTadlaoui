Projects

A Machine Learning Adaptive Application "E-Routine" to improve Learning Capacities of Children with Autism

2018 - Present

Working with children with autism is based essentially on establishing a stable routine for the children. From using daily routine cards that children relay on to predict any change in their daily life, to the use of planners in the classroom to follow the learning path individually and comprehend the next task to accomplish. "E-Routine" is a Machine Learning based application that predict the adequate routine for each child and adjust the routine based on that prediction.


Learner modeling in Adaptive Educational Hypermedia Systems

2014 - Present

The main hypothesis of this project is the use of Bayesian networks and methods as a probabilistic formalism for the management of the learner model in adaptive hypermedia. During all stages of the development of this model, since the initialization of the learner model based on the combination of Bayesian networks, and the method of stereotypes and during the update, in which we propose to integrate the networks Bayesian with the overlay model for complete probabilistic management of this model.


"E-Support" - E-Learning Support Platform

2012 - 2014

The “E-Support” project is part of the development of learning in the “Baraim Er-razi” school establishment, it is a platform which aims to computerize the course management system and school activities and the management of the whole course of learners of the 6th year of the primary school of the establishment. The purpose of the "E-Support" project is to find a solution to the problem of insufficient time to carry out activities that help learners to improve knowledge acquisition and skills development.
